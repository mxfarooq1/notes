

This chapter explained the key concepts and major types of disability income insurance, including group individual, group, and business disability policies. Let's recap the key features for each category:

|   |   |
|---|---|
|**DISABILITY INCOME**|   |
|**Disability Income Insurance**|- Replaces lost income in the event of disability<br>- Insured must be unable to perform occupational duties<br>- _Presumptive disability_ – specifies condition that qualify insured for full disability benefits<br>- _Recurrent disability_ – specifies period of time during with the recurrence of an injury or illness will be considered a continuation of a prior disability<br>- _Elimination period_ – waiting period that lasts from the onset of disability until benefit payments starts<br>- _Probationary period_ – period after the policy starts during which benefits won’ t be paid for illness-related disabilities<br>- _Benefit period_ – length of time monthly disability benefit payments last<br>- _Benefit limitations_ – maximum benefits an insurer is willing to accept for an individual risk; based on percentage of insured’s past earnings<br>- _Social Insurance Supplement (SIS) or Social Security_ Riders – supplement or replace benefits payable under Social Security Disability|
|**Business Overhead Expense Policy**|Reimburses small business owners for overhead expenses incurred while the business owner is totally disabled|
|**Business Disability Buyout Policy**|Specifies who will purchase a business partner's interest in case of disability|
|**Key Person Disability**|- Covers the potential loss of business income and the expense of hiring and training a replacement for a key person<br>- Business owns the contract, pays the premium, and is the beneficiary|
|**Group Disability**|- Short-term disability (STD) - benefit period no longer than 2 years<br>- Long-term disability (LTD):  <br>    - Elimination period may last as long as the benefit period for STD  <br>    - Benefit period may last until age 65  <br>    - Usually reserved for management employees|
