## Who could benefit from guaranteed level term life insurance

Nationwide® Guaranteed Level Term is generally a good fit for young families looking for affordable protection. It fits your life without draining your bank account.

## What is Nationwide® Guaranteed Level Term?

Nationwide Guaranteed Level Term is a term life insurance product that offers life insurance protection for a specific period of time (or term). It's available in 10-, 15-, 20- or 30-year terms.

In general, the younger and healthier you are, the lower your premium will be. Term life insurance is designed to be an inexpensive way to get a large amount of life insurance coverage.

### Key features

Affordable

An inexpensive way to get a large amount of life insurance coverage. In general, the younger and healthier you are, the lower your premium will be.

Predictable

Guaranteed level premium won’t increase for the duration of the term.

Flexible

Available in 10-, 15-, 20- or 30-year terms. Annually renewable to age 95. At the end of the term, you have the option to convert your policy to a permanent life insurance policy.
