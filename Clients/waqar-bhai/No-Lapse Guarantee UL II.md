With Nationwide No-Lapse Guarantee UL II you can:

- Replace income with an income tax-free death benefit
- Pass wealth to children, grandchildren or charitable organizations
- Help cover estate taxes, estate settlement fees and medical bills
- Set up a buy-sell agreement to protect your business
- Establish a Charitable Remainder Trust

### Key features of Nationwide No-Lapse Guarantee UL II

- A return of premium option can be added that would allow you to recover all of, or a portion of your premium if you no longer need the coverage.
- The option to have coverage up to typical retirement age or for life.
- You can access the policy death benefit while you're still living if you have a specific critical illness or become chronic or terminally ill.
