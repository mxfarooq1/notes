### Important reality check (most agents won’t say this clearly)

Guaranteed issue is **very limited** compared to normal life insurance:

- **Low coverage amounts:** usually ~$5,000 to $25,000 (sometimes up to ~$50k)
- **Higher cost per dollar** than traditional policies
- **2–3 year waiting period (graded benefit)** before full payout for natural death
- Designed mainly for **final expenses**, not income replacement

---

### Common confusion (important)

A lot of people say “no underwriting,” but mean **no medical exam**. That’s different:

- **No exam policies** → still check your health digitally (most companies)
- **Guaranteed issue** → **no checks at all** (what you asked for)
