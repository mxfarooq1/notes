### Mutual funds vs Segregated funds
- Bank offers : GIC or mutual funds
	- GIC 
		- guaranteed, 
		- no good rate of return (just 3 or 4 percent)
	- Mutual funds
		- No guarantee
		- good rate of return

- Segregated funds
	- Mixed of GIC and Mutual funds
	- Death benefit guarantee - 75% vs 100%
	- Maturity guarantee starts after 15 years
	- Reset option
	- Credit protected option
	- Bypass Probate Taxes
