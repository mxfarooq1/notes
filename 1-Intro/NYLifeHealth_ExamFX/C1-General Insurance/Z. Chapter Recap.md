

##   
Chapter Recap

This chapter was all about giving you the basics of insurance. Let's recap some of the major points:

|                                        |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **GENERAL CONCEPTS**                   |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Insurance**                          | - Transfers the risk of loss from an individual to an insurer<br>- Based on the principle of indemnity<br>- Based on the spreading of risk (risk pooling) and the law of large numbers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Hazards**                            | - Conditions that increase the probability of loss occurring<br>- 3 types of hazards:<br>    - Physical - a physical condition<br>    - Moral - a tendency toward increased risk<br>    - Morale - an indifference to loss                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Risk**                               | - Uncertainty regarding financial loss<br>- 2 types of risks:  <br>    - Pure - insurable because it involves a chance of loss only;  <br>    - Speculative - not insurable because it involves a chance of gain<br>- Methods of handling risk:  <br>    - Sharing  <br>    - Transfer  <br>    - Avoidance  <br>    - Retention  <br>    - Reduction                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Elements of Insurance Risk**         | All of the following elements apply to an insurable risk:<br><br>- _Due to chance:_ chance of loss beyond insured's control<br>- _Definite and measurable:_ loss must have definite time, place and amount<br>- _Predictable:_ number of losses must be statistically predictable<br>- _Not catastrophic:_ there must be limits that the loss can't exceed<br>- _Large exposure:_ insurer must be able to predict losses based on the law of large numbers<br>- _Randomly selected exposure:_ insurer must have a fair proportion of both good and poor risks |
| **INSURERS**                           |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Stock**                              | - Owned by stockholders<br>- Issue nonparticipating policies (nonpar)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Mutual**                             | - Owned by policyowners (policyholders)<br>- Issue participating policies (par)<br>- Pay dividends to policyholders which are a refund of excess premiums                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Fraternal Benefit Society**          | - Not for profit organization<br>- Not an insurer<br>- Formed to provide insurance benefits for members of an affiliated lodge, religious organization, or fraternal organization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **AGENT'S AUTHORITY**                  |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Express**                            | Powers specifically stated in the contract                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Implied**                            | Not specifically stated in the contract, but is assumed necessary to conduct insurance business                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Apparent**                           | The appearance of a relationship between the agent and principal based on words or actions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **COMPANY DOMICILE AND AUTHORIZATION** |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Domicile**                           | - _Domestic_ - incorporated in this state<br>- _Foreign -_ incorporated in another state of territory<br>- _Alien_ - incorporated in another country                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **Authorized/  <br>Admitted**          | - Approved by the Department of Insurance<br>- Has a Certificate of Authority                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Unauthorized/  <br>Nonadmitted**     | - No Certificate of Authority<br>- Cannot transact business in this state                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **INSURANCE CONTRACTS**                |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Elements of a Legal Contract**       | All of the following requirements apply:<br><br>- _Agreement:_ offer and acceptance<br>- _Consideration:_ premiums and representations on the part of the insured; payment of claims on the part of the insurer<br>- _Competent parties:_ of legal age, sound mental capacity, and not under the influence of drugs or alcohol<br>- _Legal purpose:_ not against public policy                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Contract Characteristics**           | - _Adhesion_ - one party prepares the contract; the other party must accept it as is<br>- _Aleatory_ - exchange of unequal amounts<br>- _Conditional_ - certain conditions must be met<br>- _Personal_ - between the policyowner and the insurance company<br>- _Unilateral -_ only one of the parties to the contract is legally bound to do anything                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Legal Interpretations**              | - _Ambiguities_ in the contract are always resolved in favor of the insured<br>- The insured can _reasonably expect coverage_ based on the agent's words and actions<br>- _Utmost good faith_ - parties rely on each other for information<br>- _Material misrepresentations_ (if intentional), _breach of warranties, concealment, fraud_ - all can void the contract<br>- _Waiver_ - voluntary act of relinquishing a legal right; _estoppel_ - consequence of a waiver                                                                                     |
