This chapter explained some of the basic principles and processes of life insurance. Let's recap them:

|                                                |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **PURCHASE OF LIFE INSURANCE**                 |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Personal Use**                               | - _Survivor protection -_ planning for survivor needs<br>- _Cash accumulation -_ permanent policies have living benefits<br>- _Estate creation -_ life insurance creates an immediate estate<br>- _Estate conservation -_ using life insurance proceeds to cover estate taxes                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **Amount of Insurance**                        | - _Human life value approach -_ potential earnings of the insured (considering salary, years to retirement, inflation)<br>- _Needs approach -_ predicted needs of the surviving family (considering debt, income, Social Security blackout, expenses)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Business Uses of Life Insurance**            | - _Key person -_ third-party ownership - business is the owner; employee is the insured<br>- _Buy-sell_ _funding -_ not insurance, but a business continuation agreement<br>- _Executive bonuses -_ employer gives the employee a wage increase in the amount of insurance premium; employee is the policyowner                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **PROCESS OF ISSUING A LIFE INSURANCE POLICY** |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Solicitation and Sales Presentations**       | - _Advertisements -_ must be truthful and not misleading<br>- _Illustrations -_ presentation of nonguaranteed elements<br>- _Buyer's guide - g_eneric information about life policies; must be provided at the time of application<br>- _Policy summary -_ description of features and benefits of the policy being issued; must be provided when the policy is delivered                                                                                                                                 |
| **Underwriting**                               | Field underwriting (by agent)<br><br>- _Application_ - completed and signed<br>- _Agent's report_ - agent's observations about the applicant that can assist in underwriting<br>- Premiums with application and conditional receipts<br><br>Company underwriting:<br><br>- Multiple sources of information (e.g., application, consumer reports, MIB report)<br>- _Selection criteria_ - cannot discriminate unfairly<br>- _Risk classification_ - 3 types of risks: standard, substandard, and preferred |
| **Premium Determination**                      | - 3 key factors for life insurance: mortality, interest and expense<br>- Premium payment mode - the higher the frequency, the higher the premium                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **Policy Issue and Delivery**                  | - Effective date of coverage - policy is delivered, and the premium is paid<br>- If the premium not paid with the application, the agent must obtain the premium and a statement of continued good health at the time of policy delivery                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
