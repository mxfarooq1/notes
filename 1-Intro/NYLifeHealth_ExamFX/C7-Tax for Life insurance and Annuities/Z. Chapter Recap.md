
This chapter explained the basic taxation principles for life insurance and annuities. Let's recap the taxable, tax-deductible and tax-free features and transactions:

|                             |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **TAXATION**                |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Life Insurance**          | - _Premiums_ - not tax deductible<br>- _Cash value_ - taxable only if the amount exceeds premiums (taxed on gain)<br>- _Policy loans_ - not taxable, interest not tax deductible<br>- _Dividends -_ not taxable as return of premium; any interest is taxable<br>- _Accelerated benefits_ - tax free<br>- _Death benefit_ - not taxable if lump-sum; any interest is taxable <br>- _Surrenders_ - taxable if the cash surrender value exceeds the amount of the premium paid |
| **Annuities**               | - _Accumulation_ - tax deferred in individual annuities, not tax deferred in corporate owned<br>- _Withdrawal of principal and interest_ - Last In, First Out basis<br>- _Lump-sum cash surrenders_ - taxable<br>- _Premature distribution_ - tax and 10% penalty<br>- _Distributions at death_ - interest taxable                                                                                                                                                           |
| **IRAs**                    | - _Contributions_ - pretax, tax deductible (must be made in cash)<br>- _Earnings_ - tax deferred<br>- _Distributions_ - taxable; 10% penalty for early withdrawals                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Roth IRAs**               | - _Contributions_ - after-tax, not tax deductible<br>- _Distributions_ - not taxable                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **OTHER RELATED CONCEPTS**  |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Rollovers and Transfers** | - Tax-free transactions<br>- Distribution of money from one qualified retirement plan to another<br>- Must be completed within 60 days<br>- If from plan to the participant, 20% of distribution is withheld<br>- If from plan to trustee, no withholdings (direct rollover)                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **1035 Exchange**           | Nontaxable if on the same life and one of the following exchanges:<br><br>- Cash value policy to another cash value policy, endowment or annuity<br>- Endowment to another endowment or annuity<br>- Annuity to another annuity                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
