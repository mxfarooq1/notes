| ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| **5 days**                                        | **License expires after renewal license is denied**                                                                             |

| **20 hours**                                      | **Prelicensing education for Life only or Accident and Health only agent or broker**                                            |
